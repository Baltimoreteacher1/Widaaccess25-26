Teacher-Friendly Data Glossary
Plain-language explanations built into the dashboard

Correlation

How strongly two variables move together. It does not prove one caused the other.

Effect size

How large a group difference is. This is often more useful than only asking whether a result is statistically significant.

Near miss

A student who did not meet the target but was within 0.2 points. These students are often the fastest instructional wins.

Opportunity score

A prioritization tool that combines missed target, gap, decline, LTML status, middle-school flag, and foundational score.
